Pankaj Ispat Ltd., the cornerstone of Pankaj Group, proudly stands as our oldest and first manufacturing unit. Situated in Gogaon, Raipur, Chhattisgarh, this facility has been instrumental in laying the foundation for our group's success and growth in the steel industry.

At Pankaj Ispat Ltd., we are committed to excellence in steel manufacturing. Our well-furnished office, state-of-the-art stores, and modern laboratories for testing chemical composition and physical strength of raw and finished materials reflect our dedication to quality and innovation. These facilities enable us to maintain the highest standards in production, ensuring that every product meets stringent quality requirements.

Under the visionary leadership of Mr. Pankaj Agrawal, Pankaj Ispat Ltd. continues to uphold the core principles of Pankaj Group: nation-building, world-class capability development, and fostering self-reliance in India. As the flagship company of our esteemed group, Pankaj Ispat Ltd. remains at the forefront of our mission to drive sustainable growth and industry excellence.
