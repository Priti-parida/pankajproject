    Introducing Gauri Ganesh Ispat Pvt Ltd: Powering the Future of Steel Production

    As we embark on a new chapter of innovation and growth, Pankaj Group is proud to introduce Gauri Ganesh Ispat Pvt Ltd, our upcoming manufacturing unit located in Tilda, Chhattisgarh. Poised to be a monumental addition to our portfolio, Gauri Ganesh Ispat Pvt Ltd is set to revolutionize the steel industry landscape with its focus on power generation and sponge iron production.

    Pankaj Group Industries Naming Four Companies

    Group Having 13 (Thirteen) Induction furnaces of 7-12 MT Capacity and 4 Automatic Rolling Mills of 18" Each and having One caster machine of 5/9 radius with two stand with direct hot charging of billet to rolling mill. Production Capacity of TMT bar is more than 1.25 Lacks MT per annual. and production Capacity of Ingots and billets are also more than 2 Lacks MT annual. Company having BIS Certification for Manufacturing of TMT Bars, Billets & Ingots Company having TNV/ISO 9001/2008 Certification for Manufacturing of quality products of TMT Bar & M.S. Billet. Group having 2500 direct & indirect workforce enjoying work with full of efficiency and peace atmosphere with long term relationship. Group having young energetic, techno and commercial experts. In our Management team enjoying with strong administrative and leadership System. The group Endeavor to Strengthen India's industrial base by adding infrastructure development through sustainable development approaches and inclusive growth. It develops its resources to improve infrastructure, education, health, water, sanitation, Environment and so on in the areas of operation.

    Our Manufacturing Units

    Pankaj Group proudly operates three distinguished manufacturing units: Pankaj Ispat Ltd, Sourabh Rolling Mills Pvt Ltd, and Alankar Alloys Pvt Ltd. Our forthcoming venture, Gauri Ganesh Ispat Pvt Ltd, poised to specialize in power and iron sponge production, represents our dedication to diversification and innovation.
