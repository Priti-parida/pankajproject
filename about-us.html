A Legacy of Strength

Shri. Om Prakash Pankaj
Founder Chairman
O.P. Pankaj Group

Over 50 years ago, Shri O.P. Pankaj had a vision of a self-reliant India. We, at JSP, are committed to realising his dreams and making India self-reliant.

2020

Resumption of CGP-DRI operations.

2022

1.4 MTPA TMT rebar mill at Angul dedicated to the Nation by former Steel Minister Ram Chandra Prasad Singh.

1952

Shri O.P. Pankaj established a steel manufacturing unit near Kolkata.

1979

JSP was incorporated in September. Thereafter, it commissioned its caster at Raigarh in October.

1999

Shares got listed through demerger.

2000

Shares of company got listed on BSE, NSE and The Stock Exchange, Ahmedabad.

2005

Pankaj Power Limited (JPL) became a subsidiary of JSP.

2008

First-ever 1000 MW thermal power plant commissioned in India at Tamnar.

2009

4.5 MTPA pellet plant commissioned at Barbil.

2010

Acquired 99.99 % stake in Shadeed Iron & Steel Co., an Oman-based company.

2012

Started commercial production of India’s widest 5.0-meter-wide plate mill at Angul.

2014

Another 1,200 MW plant was commissioned at Tamnar, and another 4.5 MTPA pellet plant was commissioned at Barbil.

2015

India’s first coal gasification plant of 2 MTPA was commissioned at Angul.

2017

Commissioned India's largest and most modern 4,554 m3 blast furnace at Angul.

2018

Raised its first equity through QIP (oversubscribed > 2.0x).

2019

Commissioned 2 coke oven batteries and received a rail order from Indian railways for 100K MT.

2020

Resumption of CGP-DRI operations.

2022

1.4 MTPA TMT rebar mill at Angul dedicated to the Nation by former Steel Minister Ram Chandra Prasad Singh.
